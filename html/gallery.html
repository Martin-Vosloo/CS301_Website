Accommodation

Rest, relax, and awaken in the heart of celebration

Farm style comfort just steps from the celebration.

Our venue features 8 elegantly appointed bed and breakfast double rooms, each with its own private en-suite bathroom. Designed for comfort, privacy, and a touch of farm style luxury, these rooms provide the perfect retreat for couples and their closest guests.

Stay on-site to fully immerse yourself in the magic of Kampvuur en Konfetti — from waking up to the sunlit gardens to winding down beside the glow of our boma fires. A delicious farm-style breakfast is included with all overnight stays.
